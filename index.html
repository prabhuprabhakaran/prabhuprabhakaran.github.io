I still remember sitting in front of my HCL Busybee computer, in earlier 1998, making it print my name and funny little symbols, limited only by my own imagination. Throughout middle and high school, I latched on to any ideas for programs and all the way. I learned C, then C++, mostly through YPK books. This enthusiasm carried me through my education at Annamalai University. I studied new languages, algorithms, compilers, higher mathematics and all pretty much. And it was here that I learned genuinely how much there was at this point to learn.

I gained a specific set of skills that allows me to deliver reliable and scalable softwares. I'm passionate about the work I do however considerably more so dedicated to developing a positive and efficient team environment. Regardless of whether in the workplace or working remotely, my door is always open to all, whether on my team or not.
