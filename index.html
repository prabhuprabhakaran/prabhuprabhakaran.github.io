about myself

Since the time I was a young child, software development has been my passion. I still remember sitting in front of my HCL Busybee computer, in earlier 1998, making it print my name for the very first time. As I went through the C tutorial, it was almost magic, being able to get the computer to do whatever I wanted, just by typing in these funny little symbols, limited only by my own imagination. Throughout middle and high school, I latched on to any ideas for programs I could get, moving from symbols and names all the way to a simple logics and calculations. I learned C, then C++, mostly through YPK books.

This enthusiasm carried me through my education at Annamalai Universiry. I studied new languages, algorithms, compilers, higher mathematics, all with pretty much the same fascination that drove me as a kid. And it was here that I learned genuinely how much there was at this point to learn.

Over the years, I gained a specific set of skills that allows me to deliver reliable and scalable softwares.

I'm passionate about the work I do however considerably more so dedicated to developing a positive and efficient team environment. regardless of whether in the workplace or working remotely, my door is always open to all, whether on my team or not.

Presently I'm in the industry about , this passion stays with me. I'm creating softwares and i'm adoring it.
